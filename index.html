The Evolution of a Trend: From Doge to Pepe to Neigh

litepaper

DOGE Era

Neigh Coin is the brainchild of a visionary team that identified a fascinating pattern in the recent trends of meme tokens. Starting with Dogecoin, the internet-famous Shiba Inu meme, we saw the power of community-driven initiatives and the potential for viral fame within the cryptocurrency space. Dogecoin's charm was not only in its technology but also in its ability to capture the imagination of a global audience.

PEPE Era

Next came Pepe the Frog token, a creation inspired by the alphabet, following the sequence from "d" (Doge) to "f" (Pepe). This trend highlighted the creative possibilities within meme coins while showcasing the strength of viral marketing and the community's role in driving these projects to unexpected heights.

Neigh Era

Enter Neigh Coin, where we continue this intriguing alphabetical journey. The letter "h" is the next step in our sequence, standing for "Horse." And who better to represent our exciting new venture than the charismatic and majestic character of the horse?
